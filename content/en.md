**Welcome.** The *Confraternita di Misericordia di Ariccia ODV* is a volunteer association that has served the community of the Castelli Romani, near Rome, **since 1994**. We stand beside people who are ill, alone or in difficulty, in the centuries-old tradition of the Italian Misericordie.

> **In an emergency always call 112** (the European emergency number).

## What we do

- **Health transport** — we take sick, elderly and disabled people to visits, treatment and hospital, by ambulance, car or wheelchair-accessible vehicle, across the Castelli Romani and throughout Italy.
- **Food bank & listening centre** — we distribute food and offer help and a listening ear to families in need.
- **First-aid training** — courses in first aid, BLSD and paediatric BLSD, open to citizens and future rescuers.
- **Civil Service** — a year of service to the community for young people aged 18–29.
- **Events & projects** — health assistance at local events, and social-inclusion projects across the area.
