*[‹ English home](/en/)*

If you have a big heart and the wish to help those in need, there is a place for you at the Misericordia di Ariccia. **No special skills are required to start: we do the training together.**

## What you can do

- **Health and social transport** — accompanying those who need care, by ambulance, car or adapted vehicle.
- **Listening centre & food bank** — welcoming families in difficulty, distributing food, listening.
- **Assistance at events** — first-aid presence at sports events and local festivals.
- **Training** — first-aid, BLSD and rescuer courses: no experience needed to begin.

## How to join

1. **Contact us or drop by** — at our premises, by phone or on WhatsApp.
2. **Submit a membership request** — a written application is enough.
3. **Start the journey** — we train you step by step until you are ready to serve.
