## Cookies and tracking

This site uses **no** profiling cookies and **no** third-party tracking or analytics. No technical or analytics cookies are installed: you can browse freely, with no consent banner.

## Data processing

The site is purely informational and **does not collect data** through online forms. If you write to us by email or phone us, we use your contact details **only to reply** and for the purpose for which you contacted us, without sharing them with third parties.
