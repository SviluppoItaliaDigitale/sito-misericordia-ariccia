*[‹ English home](/en/)*

The **Misericordia di Ariccia** has helped people **since 1994**. We are a group of volunteers who support anyone in need across the Castelli Romani, near Rome.

Our mission is to give help — both practical and human — to those who are suffering or in difficulty, inspired by the Gospel and by the *Works of Mercy*.

## What we do

- **Health transport** for sick, elderly and disabled people
- **Food bank** and distribution of essential goods to families in need
- **Listening centre** and psychological support
- **First-aid training** for citizens and rescuers
- **Civil Service** for young people, and social-inclusion projects

## A long tradition

The Misericordie are among the oldest volunteer associations in the world: a tradition of charity almost eight centuries old, born in Florence in 1244. Today more than **800** Misericordie operate across Italy; Ariccia has been part of this family since 1994.
