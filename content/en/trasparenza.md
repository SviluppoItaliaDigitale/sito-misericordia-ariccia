Here we publish the documents required under the transparency obligations for Third Sector bodies (the documents themselves are in Italian).

- **Statute** — registered version (28 May 2022) and previous version.
- **5×1000 reporting** — funds received and how they were used (2022, 2023, 2024).
- **Public contributions (Law 124/2017)** — regional and public funds received.
- **Food bank** — as a Territorial Partner Organisation of the Banco Alimentare del Lazio, within the National Programme for Inclusion. In **2025** we supported **96 people** in severe deprivation, distributing **2.61 tonnes** of food.
