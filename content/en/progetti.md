From fundraising to social inclusion, here are some of the projects through which the Misericordia di Ariccia has put solidarity into practice.

## Today

- **"The wall of kindness"** — in Velletri, with the *Rete di tutti*: leave what you can, take what you need.
- **Meal service** — in Ariccia, with **Caritas**.
- **"City with a positive impact"** — with PMG, the Municipality of Albano and Formalba.

## Past projects

- **"Dona un respiro" (2020)** — fundraising, with the Municipality of Ariccia and the Italian Red Cross, to donate a ventilator to the new Castelli hospital.
- **"Attivi Insieme / Al Tuo Servizio" (2020)** — tele-assistance and delivery of food and medicine to fragile people during the pandemic.
- **"SEMI di Inclusione" (2021)** and **"Coltivare Inclusione" (2022)** — social gardens and inclusion for disadvantaged people (Lazio Region).
- **FAMI / "Castle" projects** — support for the reception and integration of migrants (EU Asylum, Migration and Integration Fund).
- **Hospital volunteers** (until 2020) and **tele-rescue** for the elderly.
- **"MIT Link" (2024)** — sport and inclusion for 100 people with disabilities.
