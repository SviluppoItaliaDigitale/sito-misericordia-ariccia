The Misericordia di Ariccia believes that **information and services should be within everyone's reach**, including older people, people with disabilities and those with reading difficulties. We designed the site following the international **WCAG 2.1, level AA** guidelines.

## Accessibility tools

On every page you'll find the **accessibility panel** (♿ icon on the left side) and, at the top of the content, the **reading bar**. Together they let you:

- **listen** to the page read aloud (Italian speech synthesis);
- read the **easy-Italian** version;
- **enlarge the text** and use a **high-legibility** font (helpful for dyslexia);
- increase **spacing**, **highlight links**, use **high contrast**, **inverted colours** or **greyscale**;
- turn on a **reading guide**, a **large cursor** and stop animations;
- **download the text in Braille**.

## Other choices

Keyboard navigation and a "skip to content" link; **alt text** on images; clear headings and good colour contrast; a layout that adapts to every screen; respect for reduced motion; and **no cookies and no tracking**.

## Report a barrier

If you find a barrier or a page that is hard to use, tell us — we will act as soon as possible and, if needed, provide the information in an alternative format.
