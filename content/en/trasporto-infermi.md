*[‹ English home](/en/)*

When getting around becomes difficult, we are here. For over thirty years we have accompanied **sick, elderly and disabled people** with suitable vehicles, trained volunteers and full respect for the person. We operate across the **Castelli Romani** and, for long-distance journeys, throughout Italy.

## How we can help

- **Ambulance** — for those who need assistance during the journey or stretcher transport, with trained crew on board.
- **Car** — for visits, tests and discharges when an ambulance is not required: comfortable, safe and economical.
- **Wheelchair-accessible vehicle** — fitted with a lift: you board comfortably while staying in your own wheelchair.

## Book a transport

Contact us in advance with the **date and time**, **pick-up and destination**, **number of people** and any special needs (wheelchair, stretcher, companion).
