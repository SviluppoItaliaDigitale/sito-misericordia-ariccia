*[‹ English home](/en/)*

With the help of doctors, nurses and lawyers, our instructors train the future rescuers of the Misericordia. Courses are open to citizens and volunteers:

- **First aid** — the manoeuvres and good practice everyone should know.
- **Rescuer, level I and II** — the health-and-social training for ambulance crews.
- **BLSD and paediatric BLSD** — *Basic Life Support – Defibrillation*, for adults and children, taught by ARES 118-certified trainers; qualifies you to use a defibrillator.
- **Emergency-vehicle drivers** — for Misericordia volunteers.
- **Workplace safety** (Legislative Decree 81/08).
