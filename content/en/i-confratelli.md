*[‹ English home](/en/)*

The **confratelli** and **consorelle** (brothers and sisters) are the heart of the Misericordia: women and men who give their time and skills for charity and Christian fraternity, through the spiritual and corporal Works of Mercy.

According to the Statute, members have **equal rights and duties**: they take part in assemblies, vote and elect the officers. You become a member with a **written application**, on which the *Magistrato* (governing board) decides. Volunteering is **personal, spontaneous and free of charge**, and every volunteer is insured against accidents and civil liability.

## A great family

More than **800** confraternities, **670,000** members and **80,000** active volunteers across Italy. You can be part of it too: no special skills are needed to start — we train you together. [Find out how to become a volunteer](/en/diventa-volontario/).
