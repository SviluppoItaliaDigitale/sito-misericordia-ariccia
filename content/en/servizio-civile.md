Since **2015** the Misericordia di Ariccia has taken part in the **Universal Civil Service**, offering young people **aged 18–29** the chance to spend a year serving the community, in a spirit of friendship and fraternity.

Our projects aim to counter loneliness and isolation among fragile people — especially the elderly and people with disabilities — through daily support, listening and presence.

## How to apply

Applications are submitted through the national portal **Scelgo il Servizio Civile** when calls are open. Applicants must be **aged 18–29**.
