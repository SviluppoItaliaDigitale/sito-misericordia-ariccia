The **Confraternita di Misericordia di Ariccia ODV** is a volunteer association serving the community of the Castelli Romani, near Rome, **since 1994**. We stand beside people who are ill, alone or in difficulty, in the centuries-old tradition of the Italian Misericordie.

> **In an emergency always call 112** (the European emergency number).
