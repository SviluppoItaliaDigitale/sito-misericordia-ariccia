The **Works of Mercy** are the concrete acts through which the Misericordie have always put charity into practice: fourteen acts — seven **corporal** and seven **spiritual** — that answer the needs of body and soul. They are the root and the meaning of our name.

## Corporal works

Feed the hungry · give drink to the thirsty · clothe the naked · shelter the homeless · visit the sick · visit the imprisoned · bury the dead.

The Misericordia di Ariccia lives them every day through the **food bank**, **health transport**, hospital volunteers and closeness to those who suffer.

## Spiritual works

Counsel the doubtful · instruct the ignorant · admonish sinners · comfort the afflicted · forgive offences · bear wrongs patiently · pray for the living and the dead.

They are the spirit of our **listening centre** and of our daily service to the community.
