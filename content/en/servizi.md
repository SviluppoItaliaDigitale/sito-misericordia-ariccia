*[‹ English home](/en/)*

The Misericordia di Ariccia serves the community with **health and social services**, free of charge.

## Health services

- **Patient transport** by type-A ambulance, with trained crew and, when needed, medical staff on board
- **Hospital transfers** with the local health authority (ASL Roma 6)
- **Medical assistance** at sports events and public gatherings

## Social services

- **Transport for people with disabilities**, also to day centres
- **Food bank** and the annual Food Collection in local supermarkets
- **Listening centre**: help with everyday problems — paperwork, bills, access to food aid
- **Psychological support** at our premises

## Our vehicles

Two type-A ambulances, a wheelchair-accessible vehicle with a lift, and cars for social services and accompaniment.
