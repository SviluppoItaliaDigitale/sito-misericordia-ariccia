*[‹ English home](/en/)*

You can support the Misericordia di Ariccia in several ways.

## 5×1000

On the Italian tax return, in the box *"Support for volunteering and Third Sector bodies"*, **sign and write our tax code: 90031910582**. It costs you nothing, but it helps sustain volunteers, ambulances and services.

## Donation

You can make a **bank transfer** to the Confraternita di Misericordia di Ariccia — please ask us for the current IBAN, or find the details on the Italian *Sostienici* page.

## Become a volunteer

Give a little of your time: [become a volunteer](/en/diventa-volontario/). No special skills are needed to start.

Thank you for your help.
